Sustainable Intermodal Competition in Global Logistics

Abstract: This project aims to analyze the dynamics of intermodal competition within global logistics networks, focusing on enhancing sustainability and efficiency. The research will investigate policy interventions that promote environmentally friendly modes of transport while maintaining competitiveness. It includes assessing the impacts of modal shifts on greenhouse gas emissions and economic performance within the logistics sector.

Industrial Co-applicant: Umetrip

Project Amount: $500,000

Project Period: January 2025 - December 2026

Funding Scheme: Collaborative Research Grant

Funding Agent: National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)

Optimizing Green Supply Chains in Aviation

Abstract: This project aims to develop strategies for reducing the carbon footprint of aviation transport by optimizing green supply chain management. The research will focus on identifying and implementing sustainable practices, including alternative fuel technologies, operational efficiencies, and infrastructure improvements. It aims to propose policy recommendations for achieving zero-emission aviation operations.

Industrial Co-applicant: Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC)

Project Amount: $800,000

Project Period: March 2024 - February 2027

Funding Scheme: Government Research Grant

Funding Agent: Ministry of Transportation of China

Enhancing Maritime Shipping Sustainability

Abstract: This project aims to explore policy frameworks and technological innovations to enhance the sustainability of international maritime shipping and logistics. It involves assessing the environmental impact of shipping operations, analyzing the adoption of clean energy technologies, and proposing regulatory measures to promote eco-friendly practices in the maritime industry.

Industrial Co-applicant: Manchester Airport Group

Project Amount: $1,200,000

Project Period: September 2023 - August 2027

Funding Scheme: Collaborative Research Grant

Funding Agent: Research in Transportation Economics
