ABOUT

I am Dr. Wang Kun, an Assistant Professor at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Previously, I held a tenured Associate Professor position at UIBE, Beijing. I earned my BBA and MPhil from HK Polytechnic University and completed my PhD at UBC, receiving the Best Graduate Student Paper Award at the US Transportation Research Forum 58th Annual Meeting in Chicago. My research focuses on transport and logistics management, with over 80 SSCI/SCI journal publications, 30 in top-tier transportation journals, and citations exceeding 2700, with 6 papers in ESI highly cited papers.
