ABOUT

I am an Assistant Professor at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Previously, I held a tenured Associate Professor position at UIBE, Beijing. I earned my BBA and MPhil from HK Polytechnic University and completed my PhD at UBC, receiving the Best Graduate Student Paper Award at the US Transportation Research Forum 58th Annual Meeting in Chicago. My research focuses on transport and logistics management, with over 80 SSCI/SCI journal publications, 30 in top-tier transportation journals, and citations exceeding 2700, with 6 papers in ESI highly cited papers. Recognized as one of the "World's Top 2% Scientists by Stanford University," I serve as Editor of Transport Policy and on the editorial boards of prestigious journals. As a PI, I've secured NSFC grants and led consultancy projects for notable entities like CAAC, Beijing Municipal Government, Xiamen Airlines, and Manchester Airport Group, providing policy reports, some endorsed by national-level leaders.
